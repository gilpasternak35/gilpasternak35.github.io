About Me

Hi, I'm Gil

Currently a Member of Technical Staff at Fastino, where I do foundational Language Model research. My interests sit at the intersection of natural language modeling, interpretability, and (some) Machine Learning theory.

Previously, I was a Researcher at Loris (acquired by Contentsquare), one of the earliest core engineers at Great Expectations, and did AI research at NVIDIA. I earned my Bachelor's in Mathematics and Computer Science and Master's in Computer Science from UC San Diego, where I was fortunate to be advised by Misha Belkin and Ramamohan Paturi.

Outside of work, you can usually find me reading mechanistic interpretability papers, lifting heavy things, or getting into lengthy conversations with Uber drivers. I love chatting about interpretability, language models, or the weird quirks of language-based systems — if this is something that also interests you, reach out!
